    What Is Zipf?

    Zipf score measures how often a word appears in real text. A higher score (around 6 to 8) means the word is very common and usually easier to recognize. A lower score (around 3 to 4) means the word is rare and harder. Drag the range to pick how common the blanked words should be. Narrow ranges keep difficulty more consistent.
